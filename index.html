Welcome to Indhawadi

Indhawadi (इंधावडी) Gram Panchayat is a Rural Local Body in Punasa Panchayat Samiti part of East Nimar Zila Parishad. There are total 2 Villages under Indhawadi Gram Panchayat jurisdiction. Gram Panchayat is further divided into 13 Wards. Gram Panchayat has total 2 elected members by people. Gram Panchayat has total 3 schools. Gram Panchayat has total 9 full time government employees.
